Privacy Policy

Image to PDF - by playrkt

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when using our app Image to PDF.

1. Information Collection

The app does not collect any personally identifiable information. All selected images remain on your device and are never uploaded to any external server.

2. Permissions

The app only requests permissions necessary to access your camera and local storage. These permissions are required so you can select images and convert them into PDFs.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share your information with any third parties. Your privacy is our top priority.

4. Security

All data is processed locally on your device, ensuring that your information and images remain private and secure.

5. Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We recommend reviewing this page periodically to stay informed of any changes.
